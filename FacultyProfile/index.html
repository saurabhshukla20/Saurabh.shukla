About

A Leader Shaped by
Depth & Discipline

With 18 years of experience bridging academia and industry, Mr. Saurabh Shukla serves as Dean – Academics at Allenhouse Management Institutions, overseeing MBA, BBA, and BCA programs.

His journey spans two years in the corporate sector with ICICI Prudential and Future Generali before transitioning into full-time academic leadership — a path that gives him a rare dual lens: real-world rigor combined with scholarly depth.

Currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Indian Ethos from CSJM University, Kanpur, his work sits at the intersection of sustainable finance, ESG frameworks, and ancient Indian management philosophy.
